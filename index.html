Founded in 1990 in Italy brought to United States in 2015. The idea of Piccoli Morsi ("Small Bites") is the creation of the owners Monchellio and Ralphillio. It all started when Monchellio and Ralphillio met at a local dog park and struck up a conversation about their dogs and how much they enjoy spending time with them. After finding a common love for dogs and Italian food Monchellio and Ralphillio founded Piccoli Morsi, a full-service, full-course Italian restaurant that caters to canines and their owners.

​Piccoli Morsi offers a fine dining experience featuring Iron Yard Chef Michael Sweenio (not to be confused with famed Front End Designer Michael Sweeney). Piccoli Morsi is located in the beautiful Arts District in downtown Las Vegas and welcomes all breeds and their owners. We are famous in Italy for our service and special dishes designed for canines. We use the most exquisite ingredients, working with organic produce, and serving the highest quality food to the delight of our customers.

Dine in comfortable Euro-style. The restaurant has two different atmospheres: the first one is casual where the canines and their owners enjoy our beautiful outdoor beer garden, surrounded by natural landscaping with soft, twinkling lights strung through the trees, providing a subtly festive aura.

Your dog can enjoy his food next to you and at the same time our customers can appreciate the natural surroundings, and the great service we are known for. Offering complementary water bowls and home baked biscuits that are located throughout our patio area, Piccoli Morsi is the perfect place to relax, enjoy our 5-star Italian menu, and sample our special "Cane Cucina" selections--tasty treats for your 4-footed friend.

Our second option is the indoor dining room that it is more elegant, and is the favorite place for our customers to celebrate special occasions like birthday parties, anniversaries, or just a gathering of friends after the big game.
